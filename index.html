POWERFUL FEATURES

Built Specifically for Modern Agricultural Fleets

No more paper logs or messy WhatsApp bookkeeping. Manage your tractor operations using high-performance specialized tools.

Live Session Tracking

Track field sessions in real time. Log precise tractor hours, acres completed, fuel usage, and calculate costs dynamically for client billing.

Offline-First Architecture

Deep agricultural fields often have no cell reception. KrishiTrack works completely offline, queuing logs locally and auto-syncing to Cloud Firestore when you return to coverage.

Client Billing & Invoicing

Generate digital invoices for services instantly. Send them to clients via SMS, WhatsApp, or email, and monitor pending receivables in your dashboard.

Interactive Analytics

Visualize machine health, session counts, client margins, and monthly revenue metrics using gorgeous charts optimized for mobile screens.

Tax & Service Reports

Export elegant, structured PDF reports detailing services, client payments, and driver field summaries with a single tap for tax compliance.

Multi-Tenant Fleet Control

Manage multiple drivers, farm sites, and tractors from one central dashboard. Assign individual sessions and view real-time fleet assignments.
