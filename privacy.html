Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026 | Effective Date: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to KrishiTrack ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users—including tractor fleet owners, operators, and their agricultural clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our mobile and web applications (the "Service").

By accessing or using KrishiTrack, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

To provide high-quality fleet management and session tracking, we collect several categories of information:

  • Account Credentials: Full name, business details, phone number, email address, password, and profiles created for your agricultural drivers.
  • Tractor Fleet Details: Model numbers, license numbers, rates, and fuel efficiency metrics configured inside the dashboard.
  • GPS Location Mapping: Our Service accesses your device’s GPS location. This is necessary to measure field layouts, map areas ploughed, record harvesting sessions, and generate billing reports. Location mapping may occur in the background while logging active sessions.
  • Operational Session Logs: Duration of work (hours), acres worked, fuel usage, and calculated cost amounts logged by operators.
  • Offline Caching Data: When working offline, operational logs and client details are cached locally on your device (using Hive/SQLite). Once network coverage is restored, this local data is synchronized to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to operate, maintain, and optimize KrishiTrack, including:

  • Facilitating secure owner and driver authentication.
  • Tracking tractor session progress and calculating service billings.
  • Generating downloadable PDF performance, client payment, and tax reports.
  • Enabling the offline database to correctly sync and reconcile queues with Cloud Firestore.
  • Providing customer support and troubleshooting application crashes.

4. Data Storage and Security

All cloud-synchronized data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore. We implement strict administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion of your fleet records.

Your local offline data is encrypted and kept in sandbox directories on your mobile device. However, we recommend securing your device with PIN codes or biometric locks to prevent unauthorized local file access.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosures

We believe your business analytics are your own. We do not sell, lease, or distribute your operational logs or client details to third-party advertisers. We may only disclose data if required to do so by applicable laws, subpoenas, or legal processes to protect KrishiTrack's rights and assets.

6. Your Controls and Rights

You maintain full authority over your data. Inside the dashboard, you may:

  • Modify or delete driver profiles, client directories, and payment records.
  • Toggle device location and GPS tracking permissions via operating system preferences (disabling GPS mapping may limit session calculation features).
  • Request full deletion of your business subscription account and all associated cloud-synced databases by contacting support.

7. Policy Modifications

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document or by sending a push alert notification directly in the mobile app dashboard.
